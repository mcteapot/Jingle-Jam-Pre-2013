ABOUT HOLIDAY GAME JAM
It’s the most wonderful time of the year! Holiday Game Jam invites game developers to create games that celebrate the optimism, thankfulness, and love of the holiday season. Developers work over December creating games in this spirit. Each day, we will unveil a new in-progress game on our homepage. Then, December 25th, you’ll be able to donate as much or as little as you can to charity, in exchange for a bounty of fine videogame gifts!

The jam happens anytime, anywhere during December. Developers just sign up online, submit info about what they’re making, then submit it for release. We’ll be coordinating holiday parties, potlucks, movie screenings, and more that double as game jam locations throughout the month.
